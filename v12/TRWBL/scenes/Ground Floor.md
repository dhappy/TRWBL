Entry into the first public iterations of the revolution was in part through intake of substances into the [Black Team](../teams/by/color/Black%20Team)'s control.

There were two paths of induction: prescribers & patients. Prescribers were those capable of writing prescriptions for gaining access to substances which were, at the time, of limited availability. Patients received substances & either passed them on or packaged them for individual consumption.

Either way, the substances passed into the courier network and on to [[holding houses]], [[street dealers]], or [[direct sales]].

The process began with sign-up portals run on the “[dark web]()”

---

When Gold Members stumbled upon the conspiracy inadvertantly, they are offered the ability to claim their Gold Membership as well as entrance into *(at least one)* other teams. Their only primary duty, the punishment for which is assignment to the gray team, is to see that anyone currently unaware of the conspiracy remains so.

They are informed that their access to the wider network of information is limited until the end of the President to follow Trump's term.
